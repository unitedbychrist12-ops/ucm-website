# UCM 4-Step Recovery Program
## Simple Guide to Our Holistic Approach

## Overview
United by Christ Ministries helps people rebuild their lives through our 4-step program. We work with licensed professionals in each area to make sure everyone gets the help they need.

---

## STEP 1: MENTAL HEALTH & EMOTIONAL WELLNESS
**Who Helps:** Licensed counselors and therapists  
**How Long:** Ongoing support with focused 3-month start  

### What We Do
1. **Talk with a Professional Counselor**
   - Understand your challenges and strengths
   - Identify any mental health concerns
   - Check for past trauma that needs healing
   - Create a personalized treatment plan

2. **Meet with a Doctor if Needed**
   - Review any medications you might need
   - Check for other health conditions
   - Make sure you're safe and stable

3. **Get the Right Kind of Therapy**
   - Learn healthy ways to think and cope
   - Process difficult experiences safely
   - Build skills to handle stress and triggers
   - Work through trauma with proven methods

4. **Track Your Progress**
   - Regular check-ins to see how you're doing
   - Measure improvements in mood and anxiety
   - Celebrate milestones in your recovery
   - Adjust your plan as you grow stronger

---

## STEP 2: PHYSICAL HEALTH & MEDICAL CARE
**Who Helps:** Doctors and nurses  
**How Long:** Complete health check over 4 months + ongoing care

### What We Do
1. **Complete Health Check-Up**
   - Full physical exam with a doctor
   - Blood tests to check your overall health
   - Screen for diseases that need treatment
   - Heart and blood pressure monitoring
   - Address health problems from addiction

2. **Specialized Health Services**
   - Dental care and treatment
   - Eye and hearing tests
   - Nutrition counseling with a dietitian
   - Physical therapy if you need it

3. **Health Improvement Plan**
   - Manage ongoing health conditions
   - Help with taking medications correctly
   - Improve your diet and nutrition
   - Start safe exercise programs
   - Schedule regular preventive care

4. **Health Goals We Track**
   - Reach a healthy weight
   - Control blood pressure and diabetes
   - Improve overall fitness
   - Take medications as prescribed
   - Stay up-to-date with health check-ups

---

## STEP 3: FINANCIAL STABILITY & JOB TRAINING
**Who Helps:** Financial counselors and job training specialists  
**How Long:** 6 months intensive + 2 years of support

### What We Do
1. **Money Management Help**
   - Review your credit report and fix problems
   - Create a realistic budget you can follow
   - Apply for benefits you qualify for (food stamps, healthcare)
   - Set financial goals and make a plan

2. **Job Skills and Career Planning**
   - Discover your strengths and interests
   - Learn new job skills or improve existing ones
   - Get help with resumes and job interviews
   - Find training programs for good-paying careers

3. **Building Your Future**
   - Complete job readiness training
   - Earn certificates in specific job skills
   - Learn how to manage money wisely
   - Explore starting your own business if interested

4. **Success Milestones**
   - Find and keep a stable job (6+ months)
   - Earn enough to support yourself and family
   - Improve your credit score significantly
   - Pay down debt and build savings
   - Have emergency money saved ($1,000+)

---

## STEP 4: SPIRITUAL GROWTH & LIFE PURPOSE
**Who Helps:** Licensed chaplains and spiritual mentors  
**How Long:** Ongoing with structured first year

### What We Do
1. **Spiritual Assessment and Support**
   - Talk about your faith background and beliefs
   - Explore your relationship with God
   - Work through spiritual questions and struggles
   - Discover your life's purpose and calling

2. **Healing and Restoration**
   - Process grief and loss in healthy ways
   - Learn about forgiveness (giving and receiving)
   - Prepare to reconnect with community
   - Find ways to serve and help others

3. **Faith-Based Growth Activities**
   - Bible study and Christian education
   - One-on-one spiritual mentoring
   - Join worship services and church community
   - Participate in service projects and missions
   - Develop leadership skills in ministry

4. **Spiritual Growth Markers**
   - Regular participation in church community
   - Daily prayer and Bible reading habits
   - Volunteer service (4+ hours per month)
   - Mentor others and receive mentoring
   - Clear sense of life purpose and direction

---

## HOW ALL 4 STEPS WORK TOGETHER

### Team Approach
- **Weekly meetings** between all your helpers
- **Monthly check-ins** with you to review progress
- **Quarterly assessments** to measure success
- **Annual reviews** to celebrate growth and plan ahead

### Professional Standards
- All services provided by licensed professionals
- Regular training to stay current with best practices
- Confidential record keeping following all privacy laws
- Proven methods that have helped others succeed

### Your Journey Documentation
- Private, secure record keeping
- Regular progress notes and updates
- Measurable goals and achievements
- Outcome tracking to show your success

---

## SIMPLE ASSESSMENT PROCESS

### When You Start (Professional Evaluation)
1. **Mental Health:** Talk with counselors, take assessments
2. **Physical Health:** Complete medical exam, lab tests, specialist visits
3. **Financial:** Review credit, test job skills, check benefit eligibility
4. **Spiritual:** Explore faith, assess spiritual needs, community readiness

### Monthly Check-Ins (Track Your Progress)
- Review goals in each of the 4 areas
- Celebrate successes and address challenges
- Update your plan as you grow and change
- Make sure you're getting the support you need

### Long-Term Success (Ongoing Evaluation)
- 6-month comprehensive review
- 1-year celebration and assessment
- 2-year follow-up and continued support
- 5-year success story documentation

**This program ensures you get professional help in all areas of life while keeping faith and hope at the center of your recovery journey.**
