# UCM AI PHONE CALLING SYSTEM
**Automated Donation Outreach with Human-Like Conversations**

---

## SYSTEM OVERVIEW

### AI CALLING OBJECTIVES
1. **Primary Goal:** Secure donations for UCM's addiction recovery programs
2. **Secondary Goal:** Direct prospects to ucm.live for online donations
3. **Tertiary Goal:** Offer cost-saving services as alternative support
4. **Data Goal:** Build donor database and track engagement

### COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS
- **Do Not Call Registry compliance** (essential for nonprofits)
- **Caller ID transparency** (must show UCM information)
- **Opt-out mechanisms** (immediate removal from calling list)
- **Recording disclosure** (if calls are recorded)
- **State-specific regulations** (CA/TX telemarketing laws)

## AI CONVERSATION SCRIPT

### **OPENING (WARM & TRANSPARENT)**
*"Hi, this is Sarah calling on behalf of United by Christ Ministries, a local nonprofit helping people overcome addiction and find stable housing. I'm an AI assistant helping us reach more people in our community. Do you have just a minute to hear about the important work we're doing?"*

**[PAUSE FOR RESPONSE]**

### **MISSION PRESENTATION**
*"UCM operates a comprehensive 4-step program that has helped over 500 people in our area overcome addiction, find housing, and rebuild their lives. We provide addiction counseling, job training, housing assistance, and spiritual support to help people get back on their feet and reunite with their families."*

### **DONATION REQUEST (PRIMARY)**
*"We rely entirely on community support to fund these life-changing programs. Would you be willing to make a donation to help us continue this work? Even $25 can provide meals for someone for three days, and $100 can fund a week of housing assistance."*

**[IF INTERESTED]**
*"That's wonderful! I can direct you to our secure donation website at ucm.live where you can make your donation safely online. Would you like me to text you the link, or would you prefer I stay on the line while you visit the site?"*

### **OBJECTION HANDLING**

**"I don't have money to donate right now"**
*"I completely understand - times are challenging for many families. We actually have a unique way you can still support our mission without any out-of-pocket cost. We partner with a service that helps families reduce their monthly bills on internet, phones, and energy. When you save money through our partner, a portion supports our programs. Would you be interested in learning how much you might save on your monthly bills?"*

**"I already give to other charities"**
*"That's wonderful - thank you for supporting charitable work. UCM focuses specifically on addiction recovery and homelessness right here in our local community, so we're addressing issues that directly affect our neighbors. Even a small donation makes a huge difference, or you might be interested in our cost-saving services that help you support our mission while reducing your expenses."*

**"I don't support religious organizations"**
*"I appreciate your honesty. While UCM is faith-based, we serve people of all backgrounds and beliefs. Our programs focus on practical help - housing, job training, addiction counseling - regardless of someone's religious views. We believe everyone deserves a second chance and the support they need to rebuild their lives."*

### **SERVICE REFERRAL (SECONDARY OPTION)**
*"If a donation isn't possible right now, we have a community partnership that might help you save money on your monthly bills. Our partner helps families reduce costs on internet, phones, solar, and other services. Many people save $50-200 monthly, and when you use these services, a portion supports our programs. Would you like to learn more about potential savings?"*

### **WEBSITE DIRECTION**
*"You can learn more about our programs and make a secure donation at ucm.live - that's U-C-M dot live. I can also text you the link right now if that's easier. Would you like me to send that to this number?"*

### **CLOSING**
*"Thank you so much for your time and for caring about our community. Whether you're able to donate or not, please keep our mission in your thoughts and prayers. If you or someone you know ever needs our help, don't hesitate to reach out. Have a blessed day!"*

   - Get local CA/TX numbers for better answer rates
   - Set up caller ID: "United by Christ Ministries"

   - Scrub all calling lists against DNC registry
   - Maintain internal opt-out list
   - Update lists monthly

### **WEBSITE INTEGRATION**
1. **Donation Landing Page**
   - Create ucm.live/donate for easy access
   - Mobile-optimized donation forms
   - Multiple payment options (credit, PayPal, etc.)

2. **Call Tracking**
   - UTM parameters for AI call traffic
   - Conversion tracking from calls to donations
   - A/B testing different scripts

### **CRM INTEGRATION**
1. **Lead Management**
   - Capture all call data (answered, voicemail, interested)
   - Track donation amounts and follow-up needed
   - Segment lists for different campaigns

2. **Follow-Up Automation**
   - Email sequences for interested prospects
   - Text message reminders for donation links
   - Thank you messages for donors
