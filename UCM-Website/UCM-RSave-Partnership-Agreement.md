# COMMUNITY PARTNERSHIP AGREEMENT
**Between United by Christ Ministries (UCM) and R Save LLC**

## AGREEMENT OVERVIEW
This Community Partnership Agreement establishes a compliant relationship between UCM (501c3 nonprofit) and R Save LLC (for-profit entity) to support UCM's charitable mission while maintaining IRS compliance.

---

## ARTICLE I: PARTNERSHIP PURPOSE

### 1.1 UCM'S CHARITABLE PURPOSE
UCM operates exclusively for charitable purposes including:
- Addiction recovery and treatment programs
- Housing assistance for homeless individuals and families
- Financial literacy and community education programs
- Spiritual development and faith-based support services

### 1.2 COMMUNITY PARTNERSHIP OBJECTIVE
This partnership supports UCM's charitable mission by:
- Providing financial literacy education to the community
- Connecting community members with cost-saving resources
- Generating donations for UCM's charitable programs
- Expanding UCM's community outreach capabilities

### 1.3 R SAVE'S ROLE
R Save operates as an independent for-profit entity providing:
- Residential and commercial internet services
- Solar installation and energy services
- Cellular phone and telecommunications services
- Merchant services for businesses
- Other home and business services

---

## ARTICLE II: OPERATIONAL STRUCTURE

### 2.1 SEPARATE ENTITIES
- UCM and R Save maintain complete operational independence
- No shared employees, management, or decision-making authority
- Separate accounting, banking, and financial systems
- Independent legal and tax obligations

### 2.2 UCM ACTIVITIES (CHARITABLE/EDUCATIONAL)
UCM may engage in the following activities:
- **Financial Wellness Education:** Teaching community members about reducing monthly expenses
- **Community Outreach:** Connecting families with resources to improve financial stability
- **Referral Services:** Directing community members to appropriate service providers
- **Impact Reporting:** Documenting community benefit and program effectiveness

### 2.3 R SAVE ACTIVITIES (COMMERCIAL)
R Save operates independently and may:
- Market and sell services to consumers and businesses
- Employ sales representatives and support staff
- Provide customer service and technical support
- Handle all commercial transactions and contracts

---

## ARTICLE III: DONATION STRUCTURE

### 3.1 REFERRAL TRACKING
R Save will implement systems to track the source of customer referrals, including:
- Direct marketing and advertising
- UCM community referrals and educational programs
- Other partnership and referral sources
- Online tracking through website analytics

### 3.2 DONATION CALCULATION
For customers referred through UCM's educational programs:
- R Save will donate **80% of net profits** from such customers to UCM
- Net profits calculated as gross revenue minus direct costs and reasonable business expenses
- Donations calculated monthly based on completed transactions
- Minimum donation period of 12 months per customer relationship

### 3.3 DONATION PROCESS
- Monthly calculation of donations owed to UCM
- Written report provided to UCM detailing calculation methodology
- Donations transferred within 30 days of month-end
- Annual reconciliation and audit rights for UCM

---

## ARTICLE IV: EMPLOYMENT STRUCTURE

### 4.1 UCM EMPLOYEES
UCM may employ staff for charitable and educational purposes:

**Position:** Community Outreach Coordinator
- **Salary:** $4,000-6,000 per month (fixed compensation)
- **Benefits:** Health insurance, retirement plan, paid time off
- **Duties:** Financial literacy education, community relationship building, referral coordination
- **Reporting:** UCM Board of Directors and Executive Leadership
- **Performance Metrics:** Community engagement, educational program effectiveness, relationship development

**Annual Performance Bonus Structure:**
- Based on overall UCM fundraising goals (all sources)
- Maximum 25% of annual salary
- Determined by UCM Board based on charitable mission advancement
- NOT tied to specific R Save sales or commissions

### 4.2 R SAVE EMPLOYEES
R Save independently employs sales and support staff:

**Position:** Service Consultant / Sales Representative
- **Compensation:** Commission-based ($225+ per sale)
- **Status:** Independent contractor or employee of R Save
- **Duties:** Sales presentations, customer service, contract completion
- **Reporting:** R Save management and leadership
- **Performance Metrics:** Sales volume, customer satisfaction, revenue generation

### 4.3 PROHIBITED ARRANGEMENTS
- No shared employees between UCM and R Save
- No commission-based compensation for UCM staff
- No salary-based compensation for R Save staff from UCM funds
- No cross-employment or dual reporting relationships

---

## ARTICLE V: COMPLIANCE SAFEGUARDS

### 5.1 IRS COMPLIANCE
Both parties commit to maintaining compliance with:
- UCM's 501(c)(3) tax-exempt status requirements
- Private benefit and private inurement prohibitions
- Substantial non-exempt purpose limitations
- Reasonable compensation standards

### 5.2 DOCUMENTATION REQUIREMENTS
- Separate books and records for each entity
- Clear documentation of referral sources and donation calculations
- Annual compliance review by qualified legal counsel
- Regular board oversight and approval of partnership terms

### 5.3 TRANSPARENCY MEASURES
- Public disclosure of partnership on both entities' websites
- Clear explanation of donation structure to community members
- Annual impact reporting on charitable benefits generated
- Open communication about partnership terms and benefits

---

## ARTICLE VI: MESSAGING AND MARKETING

### 6.1 UCM MESSAGING (EDUCATIONAL FOCUS)
UCM's communications will emphasize:
- Financial literacy and community education mission
- Helping families reduce monthly expenses and improve financial stability
- Connecting community members with appropriate resources
- Supporting UCM's charitable programs through community partnerships

**Sample UCM Message:**
*"UCM's Financial Wellness Program helps families in our community reduce monthly expenses and improve financial stability. Through community partnerships, we connect families with resources that can save money while generating support for our addiction recovery and housing programs."*

### 6.2 R SAVE MESSAGING (COMMERCIAL FOCUS)
R Save's communications will emphasize:
- Quality services at competitive rates
- Professional customer service and technical support
- Community partnership and charitable giving commitment
- Supporting local nonprofit organizations

**Sample R Save Message:**
*"R Save provides quality internet, solar, and telecommunications services at competitive rates. We're proud to support United by Christ Ministries' mission by donating a portion of our profits to their addiction recovery and housing programs."*

### 6.3 JOINT MESSAGING GUIDELINES
When referencing the partnership:
- Emphasize community benefit and charitable mission support
- Avoid language suggesting UCM operates for commercial purposes
- Clearly distinguish between educational/referral activities and commercial sales
- Maintain focus on UCM's charitable mission as primary purpose

---

## ARTICLE VII: PERFORMANCE METRICS

### 7.1 UCM SUCCESS METRICS
- Number of community members served through financial wellness programs
- Effectiveness of educational workshops and outreach activities
- Community feedback and satisfaction with referral services
- Total charitable donations received (from all sources)
- Impact on UCM's charitable mission advancement

### 7.2 R SAVE SUCCESS METRICS
- Customer acquisition and retention rates
- Service quality and customer satisfaction scores
- Revenue growth and business development
- Charitable donations provided to community partners
- Community reputation and brand recognition

### 7.3 PARTNERSHIP SUCCESS METRICS
- Total donations generated for UCM through partnership
- Number of community members benefiting from cost savings
- Expansion of UCM's community outreach capabilities
- Long-term sustainability of partnership model
- Compliance with all legal and regulatory requirements

---

## ARTICLE VIII: TERM AND TERMINATION

### 8.1 INITIAL TERM
This agreement shall remain in effect for an initial term of three (3) years from the effective date, with automatic renewal for additional one-year periods unless terminated by either party.

### 8.2 TERMINATION RIGHTS
Either party may terminate this agreement:
- With 90 days written notice for any reason
- Immediately for material breach of agreement terms
- Immediately if continuation would jeopardize UCM's tax-exempt status
- Upon mutual agreement of both parties

### 8.3 POST-TERMINATION OBLIGATIONS
- Final donation calculation and payment within 60 days
- Return of confidential information and materials
- Cessation of joint marketing and promotional activities
- Continued compliance with confidentiality obligations

---

## ARTICLE IX: LEGAL PROVISIONS

### 9.1 GOVERNING LAW
This agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California and applicable federal regulations governing 501(c)(3) organizations.

### 9.2 DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Any disputes shall be resolved through:
1. Good faith negotiation between parties
2. Mediation with qualified nonprofit law mediator
3. Binding arbitration if mediation unsuccessful

### 9.3 AMENDMENT PROCESS
This agreement may only be amended:
- In writing signed by authorized representatives of both parties
- With approval of UCM's Board of Directors
- After legal review to ensure continued IRS compliance
- With 30 days advance notice to all stakeholders
