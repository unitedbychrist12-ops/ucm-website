## COMPLIANCE & MESSAGING GUIDELINES

### MAINTAIN UCM'S MISSION FOCUS
- Always lead with UCM's mission
- R Save as a tool to support the ministry
- Avoid making it seem purely commercial
- Emphasize the "support while you save" message

### AUTHENTIC STORYTELLING
- Real customer testimonials only
- Genuine rep success stories
- Actual UCM impact data
- Transparent about the partnership

### REGULATORY COMPLIANCE
- Include earnings disclaimers on recruitment posts
- Follow FTC guidelines for endorsements
- Maintain separation between ministry and business
- Clear disclosure of the partnership relationship
