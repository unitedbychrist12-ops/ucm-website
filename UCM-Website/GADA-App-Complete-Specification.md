**Gambling, Alcohol, Drug & Addicts in Abstinence - Community Recovery Platform**

## APP OVERVIEW

### **MISSION:**
Create a supportive community platform where people in recovery can connect, track progress, receive encouragement, and support each other through their journey to sobriety and wellness.

### **TARGET USERS:**
- **Primary:** People in recovery from gambling, alcohol, drugs, or other addictions
- **Secondary:** Sponsors, mentors, family members, counselors
- **Tertiary:** UCM program participants and alumni
