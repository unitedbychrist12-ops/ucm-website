# UCM 4-Step Holistic Recovery Program Framework
## Professionally Integrated Assessment & Treatment Model

## Overview
United by Christ Ministries' 4-Step Program provides comprehensive evaluation and treatment through licensed professionals in each domain, ensuring evidence-based, measurable outcomes.

---

## STEP 1: MENTAL HEALTH & PSYCHOLOGICAL WELLNESS
**Professional Lead:** Licensed Clinical Psychologist/Psychiatrist  
**Duration:** Ongoing with intensive 90-day foundation  

### Professional Diagnostic Assessment
1. **Clinical Evaluation (Licensed Psychologist)**
   - DSM-5 diagnostic assessment
   - Addiction Severity Index (ASI)
   - Trauma assessment (ACE Score, PCL-5)
   - Cognitive assessment (MMSE, MoCA)
   - Personality assessment (MMPI-2)

2. **Psychiatric Evaluation (MD/Psychiatrist)**
   - Medication assessment and management
   - Co-occurring disorder diagnosis
   - Neurological screening
   - Risk assessment protocols

3. **Evidence-Based Treatment Interventions**
   - Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)
   - Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT)
   - Trauma-Informed Care (TIC)
   - Motivational Interviewing (MI)
   - EMDR for trauma processing

4. **Measurable Outcomes & Progress Indicators**
   - PHQ-9 (depression) - target <5
   - GAD-7 (anxiety) - target <5
   - AUDIT/DAST (substance use) - sustained sobriety
   - Treatment engagement scores
   - Crisis episode reduction (90% decrease)

---

## STEP 2: PHYSICAL HEALTH & MEDICAL WELLNESS
**Professional Lead:** Licensed Physician (MD) & Registered Nurse  
**Duration:** Comprehensive 120-day restoration + ongoing maintenance

### Medical Diagnostic Assessment
1. **Comprehensive Medical Evaluation (MD)**
   - Complete physical examination
   - Laboratory workup (CBC, CMP, lipid panel, HbA1c)
   - Infectious disease screening (HIV, Hepatitis, TB)
   - Cardiovascular assessment (EKG, blood pressure monitoring)
   - Substance abuse medical complications

2. **Specialized Health Assessments**
   - Dental examination and treatment plan
   - Vision and hearing screening
   - Nutritional assessment (RD consultation)
   - Physical therapy evaluation if needed

3. **Treatment & Wellness Interventions**
   - Chronic disease management protocols
   - Medication adherence programs
   - Nutritional rehabilitation
   - Exercise physiology programs
   - Preventive care scheduling

4. **Measurable Health Outcomes**
   - BMI normalization (18.5-24.9)
   - Blood pressure control (<130/80)
   - Diabetes management (HbA1c <7%)
   - Substance-related health improvement
   - Medication compliance (>90%)

---

## STEP 3: FINANCIAL LITERACY & ECONOMIC STABILITY
**Professional Lead:** Certified Financial Counselor & Vocational Rehabilitation Specialist  
**Duration:** 180-day intensive + 2-year follow-up

### Financial & Vocational Assessment
1. **Economic Evaluation (Certified Financial Counselor)**
   - Credit report analysis and repair plan
   - Debt-to-income ratio assessment
   - Benefits eligibility evaluation (SNAP, Medicaid, housing)
   - Financial goal setting and budgeting capacity

2. **Vocational Assessment (Licensed Vocational Counselor)**
   - Skills inventory and aptitude testing
   - Work history and barrier analysis
   - Career interest assessment (Strong Interest Inventory)
   - Accommodation needs evaluation

3. **Professional Development Interventions**
   - Certified job readiness training
   - Industry-specific skill certification
   - Financial literacy certification program
   - Entrepreneurship development (if applicable)

4. **Economic Success Metrics**
   - Employment stability (6+ months, 30+ hours/week)
   - Income sufficiency (>200% federal poverty level)
   - Credit score improvement (100+ point increase)
   - Debt reduction (50% within 2 years)
   - Emergency savings establishment ($1,000 minimum)

---

## STEP 4: SPIRITUAL DEVELOPMENT & PURPOSE INTEGRATION
**Professional Lead:** Licensed Clinical Chaplain & Spiritual Director  
**Duration:** Ongoing with structured 12-month foundation

### Spiritual & Purpose Assessment
1. **Spiritual Evaluation (Licensed Chaplain)**
   - Spiritual history and religious background
   - Faith development assessment (Fowler's stages)
   - Spiritual coping inventory
   - Religious/spiritual struggle assessment
   - Purpose in life evaluation (PIL test)

2. **Pastoral Care Assessment**
   - Grief and loss processing
   - Forgiveness and reconciliation needs
   - Community integration readiness
   - Service and calling discernment

3. **Faith-Based Interventions**
   - Structured Bible study and theology education
   - Spiritual direction and mentorship
   - Community worship integration
   - Service learning and mission participation
   - Leadership development in ministry

4. **Spiritual Growth Indicators**
   - Faith community active participation (weekly)
   - Spiritual practices consistency (daily prayer/study)
   - Service hours completion (4+ hours/month)
   - Mentorship engagement (giving and receiving)
   - Life purpose clarity and action plan

## INTEGRATED PROFESSIONAL ASSESSMENT PROTOCOL

### Multi-Disciplinary Team Approach
- **Weekly case conferences** with all professional leads
- **Monthly comprehensive reviews** with participant
- **Quarterly outcome assessments** with external evaluation
- **Annual program effectiveness analysis**

### Professional Licensing Requirements
- All assessments conducted by licensed professionals
- Supervision and consultation protocols established
- Continuing education requirements maintained
- Evidence-based practice standards followed

### Clinical Documentation Standards
- HIPAA-compliant record keeping
- Standardized assessment instruments
- Progress note requirements
- Outcome measurement protocols

---

## ENHANCED ASSESSMENT TOOLS & DIAGNOSTICS

### Intake Battery (Professional Administration)
1. **Psychological:** MMPI-2, Beck inventories, trauma assessments
2. **Medical:** Comprehensive labs, physical exam, specialist referrals
3. **Financial:** Credit analysis, vocational testing, benefits screening
4. **Spiritual:** Faith development scales, purpose assessments, community readiness

### Progress Monitoring (Monthly Professional Review)
- Standardized outcome measures for each domain
- Goal attainment scaling
- Risk assessment updates
- Treatment plan modifications

### Success Validation (External Professional Evaluation)
- 6-month independent assessment
- 1-year comprehensive evaluation
- 2-year follow-up study
- 5-year longitudinal outcome tracking

---

This framework ensures that UCM's 4-step program meets professional clinical standards while maintaining the faith-based, holistic approach that makes it unique.
