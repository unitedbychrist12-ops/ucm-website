## UCM COMMUNITY OUTREACH COORDINATOR
**Employment Agreement Template**

### POSITION OVERVIEW
**Job Title:** Community Outreach Coordinator - Financial Wellness Program
**Department:** Community Services
**Reports To:** UCM Executive Director
**Employment Type:** Full-time, Exempt
**FLSA Status:** Exempt

### POSITION PURPOSE
Support UCM's charitable mission by providing financial literacy education to community members, connecting families with resources to improve financial stability, and expanding UCM's community outreach capabilities.

### ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

**Primary Responsibilities (70% of time):**
1. **Financial Literacy Education**
   - Develop and conduct workshops on budgeting, expense reduction, and financial planning
   - Create educational materials about reducing monthly household expenses
   - Provide one-on-one financial counseling to community members
   - Track and report on educational program effectiveness

2. **Community Relationship Building**
   - Build relationships with community members and families
   - Identify families in need of financial assistance and education
   - Connect community members with appropriate resources and services
   - Maintain ongoing relationships to support long-term financial stability

3. **Outreach Program Development**
   - Plan and execute community outreach events and programs
   - Collaborate with local organizations and community partners
   - Develop strategies to reach underserved populations
   - Evaluate and improve outreach program effectiveness

**Secondary Responsibilities (30% of time):**
4. **Resource Coordination and Referrals**
   - Maintain database of community resources and service providers
   - Make appropriate referrals to help community members reduce expenses
   - Follow up on referrals to ensure community member satisfaction
   - Document referral outcomes and community impact

5. **Program Administration and Reporting**
   - Maintain accurate records of community interactions and program participation
   - Prepare monthly reports on program activities and outcomes
   - Assist with grant applications and donor reporting related to community programs
   - Support UCM's overall fundraising and development activities

### REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS

**Education:**
- Bachelor's degree in Social Work, Community Development, Business, or related field
- OR equivalent combination of education and relevant experience

**Experience:**
- Minimum 2 years experience in community outreach, social services, or related field
- Experience with financial counseling or education preferred
- Nonprofit sector experience preferred

**Skills and Abilities:**
- Excellent interpersonal and communication skills
- Ability to work with diverse populations and sensitive situations
- Strong organizational and time management skills
- Proficiency with computer applications and database management
- Bilingual (English/Spanish) preferred

**Personal Characteristics:**
- Commitment to UCM's mission and Christian values
- Compassionate and non-judgmental approach to community service
- Professional demeanor and strong ethical standards
- Ability to maintain confidentiality and appropriate boundaries

### COMPENSATION AND BENEFITS

**Base Salary:** $48,000 - $72,000 annually (based on experience)
- Starting range: $4,000 - $6,000 per month
- Annual salary review with potential increases based on performance and budget

**Benefits Package:**
- Health insurance (UCM pays 80% of premium)
- Dental and vision insurance options
- 403(b) retirement plan with UCM matching contribution
- Paid time off: 15 days vacation, 10 sick days, 12 holidays
- Professional development allowance: $1,000 annually

**Performance Bonus Opportunity:**
- Annual performance bonus up to 25% of base salary
- Based on overall UCM fundraising goals and mission advancement
- Determined by UCM Board of Directors
- NOT tied to specific referrals or commercial activities

### PERFORMANCE EVALUATION CRITERIA

**Annual Performance Metrics:**
1. **Community Engagement (40%)**
   - Number of community members served through programs
   - Quality of relationships built and maintained
   - Community feedback and satisfaction scores
   - Expansion of outreach to new populations

2. **Educational Program Effectiveness (30%)**
   - Workshop attendance and completion rates
   - Participant feedback and learning outcomes
   - Development of new educational materials and programs
   - Innovation in program delivery methods

3. **Resource Coordination Success (20%)**
   - Appropriateness and effectiveness of referrals made
   - Follow-up completion rates and outcomes
   - Community member satisfaction with referral services
   - Expansion of resource network and partnerships

4. **Professional Development and Mission Alignment (10%)**
   - Participation in training and professional development
   - Demonstration of UCM values and mission commitment
   - Collaboration with UCM team and leadership
   - Contribution to overall organizational goals

### COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS

**IRS Compliance Obligations:**
- Compensation must remain reasonable for nonprofit sector
- No commission-based or percentage-based compensation
- Performance bonuses tied to charitable mission advancement only
- Annual documentation of compensation reasonableness

**Prohibited Activities:**
- Direct sales activities or commercial transactions
- Commission-based compensation from any source
- Primary focus on commercial rather than charitable activities
- Personal financial benefit from referral activities

**Required Documentation:**
- Monthly activity reports documenting charitable purpose
- Annual performance evaluation with mission-focused metrics
- Compliance certification regarding prohibited activities
- Professional development and training records

## R SAVE SALES REPRESENTATIVE
**Independent Contractor Agreement Template**

### POSITION OVERVIEW
**Job Title:** Service Consultant / Sales Representative
**Company:** R Save LLC
**Relationship:** Independent Contractor
**Compensation:** Commission-Based

### POSITION PURPOSE
Generate revenue for R Save LLC by marketing and selling internet, solar, telecommunications, and other services to residential and commercial customers while maintaining professional standards and customer satisfaction.

### SCOPE OF WORK

**Primary Responsibilities:**
1. **Sales and Marketing Activities**
   - Prospect for new customers through various channels
   - Conduct sales presentations and product demonstrations
   - Close sales and complete customer contracts
   - Meet or exceed monthly sales targets

2. **Customer Service and Support**
   - Provide excellent customer service throughout sales process
   - Address customer questions and concerns professionally
   - Coordinate with technical teams for service installation
   - Maintain ongoing customer relationships for additional sales opportunities

3. **Business Development**
   - Identify new market opportunities and customer segments
   - Develop and maintain professional network for referrals
   - Participate in trade shows, community events, and marketing activities
   - Provide feedback to management on market conditions and opportunities

### COMPENSATION STRUCTURE

**Commission Rates:**
- Fiber Internet: $225 per activation
- Solar Installation: $1,000 - $5,000 per project (based on size)
- Roofing Projects: $500 - $3,000 per project
- Merchant Services: $200 - $1,000 per account + residuals
- Cell Phone Plans: $50 - $200 per activation

**Payment Terms:**
- Commissions paid monthly within 30 days of service activation
- Direct deposit or check payment options available
- Annual 1099 tax reporting for independent contractors

**Performance Bonuses:**
- Monthly volume bonuses for exceeding targets
- Quarterly performance incentives for top performers
- Annual recognition and reward programs
- Team building and leadership opportunities

### PERFORMANCE EXPECTATIONS

**Monthly Targets (Full-Time):**
- Minimum 10 sales per month across all service categories
- Customer satisfaction rating of 4.5+ out of 5
- Professional conduct and compliance with company policies
- Participation in training and development programs

**Quality Standards:**
- Accurate and complete customer information collection
- Honest and transparent sales presentations
- Timely follow-up on customer inquiries and concerns
- Compliance with all applicable laws and regulations

### INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR TERMS

**Contractor Status:**
- No employee benefits provided by R Save
- Responsible for own taxes, insurance, and business expenses
- Flexible schedule and work location (within assigned territory)
- Use of own vehicle and equipment (with expense reimbursement available)

**Territory and Exclusivity:**
- Assigned geographic territory or customer segment
- Non-compete restrictions during contract term
- Confidentiality obligations regarding customer information
- Compliance with R Save policies and procedures

## DUAL-ROLE COMPLIANCE STRUCTURE

### SCENARIO: UCM SUPPORTER BECOMES R SAVE REP

**Compliant Approach:**
1. **Separate Employment:** Individual works for UCM as Community Outreach Coordinator (salary-based)
2. **Independent Contracting:** Same individual contracts with R Save as Sales Representative (commission-based)
3. **Clear Role Separation:** Different duties, reporting, and compensation for each role
4. **Time Allocation:** Specific hours dedicated to each role with clear documentation

**Required Safeguards:**
- Written agreements clearly defining each role and compensation
- Separate time tracking and activity reporting for each position
- UCM Board approval of any dual-role arrangements
- Annual compliance review to ensure no conflicts of interest

**Documentation Requirements:**
- Dual employment disclosure and approval forms
- Separate performance evaluations for each role
- Clear allocation of time and activities between roles
- Regular compliance monitoring and reporting

## RECRUITMENT AND HIRING PROCESS

### UCM COMMUNITY OUTREACH COORDINATOR

**Recruitment Strategy:**
- Post position on nonprofit job boards and UCM website
- Emphasize mission-driven opportunity and community impact
- Target candidates with social services or community development background
- Include salary range and benefits in job posting

**Interview Process:**
1. Application review focusing on mission alignment and relevant experience
2. Phone screening to assess communication skills and interest
3. In-person interview with UCM leadership team
4. Reference checks and background verification
5. Board approval for final hiring decision

**Onboarding Process:**
- Comprehensive orientation to UCM mission and programs
- Training on financial literacy education and community outreach
- Introduction to community partners and resources
- 90-day probationary period with regular check-ins

### R SAVE SALES REPRESENTATIVES

**Recruitment Strategy:**
- Post positions on sales job boards and industry websites
- Emphasize high earning potential and flexible schedule
- Target experienced sales professionals and motivated beginners
- Include commission structure and performance expectations

**Selection Process:**
1. Application review focusing on sales experience and motivation
2. Phone screening to assess communication and sales skills
3. In-person or video interview with sales management
4. Skills assessment or role-playing exercise
5. Reference checks and background verification

**Training Program:**
- Product knowledge and sales techniques training
- Customer service and professional standards
- Territory assignment and goal setting
- Ongoing coaching and performance support
