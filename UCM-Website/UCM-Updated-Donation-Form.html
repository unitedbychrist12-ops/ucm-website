Our Mission & Testimony

United by Christ Ministries is dedicated to reach out to the Alcoholic, Drug Addict, Gang Member, Felon, Incarcerated, Homeless Adults & Families and any Souls in need of Gods Love, Grace and Mercy. The Ministries goal is to help save souls through Christ, rehabilitate, house, educate and prepare everyone for a better future...

I once was lost in search for answers and needed a drastic change in my life. Someone once asked me, how would you like to reach out to alcoholics, drug addicts and gang members out in the streets and I replied I would love to. So I attended a Family Assembly with Barrios Unidos En Cristo in Long Beach at St. Athanasius Catholic Church. This is where my journey in Christ begun. God blessed me that day and I had my first encounter with our Lord Jesus Christ. I made a commitment with God that day; to help other people like me who were struggling to make a change in they're life due to alcohol, drugs and violence...

The Holy Spirit immediately started doing a radical change in me and my life. I started to serve in this Ministry called Barrios Unidos En Cristo. (Neighborhoods United in Christ). Until I had to move due to a work related transfer. I had moved for a better opportunity. Financially I was doing great but spiritually I was going through some difficult times and tribulations. I was only a baby in the faith away from the Ministry and Church Community. It became a struggle to stay connected and I became spiritually devastated in need of Gods Grace more than ever. God never gave up on me and strengthened me through my hardships; which now serves as my testimonial.

Years later I felt the urge and the calling to start up a similar Ministry and keep my promise that I once made to God... Through United by Christ Ministries, God has bestowed big goals and the vision is to go Nation Wide and eventually WorldWide with our Ministry to help every person and family in need... We will be launching large marketing campaigns to hit The Ministries goals but we can't get there without you and or the smaller steps we need to take. All of your prayers will help the Ministries initial launch and future success.

We are still in the infinite stages of our Ministry and have made a promise to do something special to all who help contribute to the cause. We will keep you and your family in our prayers and add your name to the United by Christ Ministries Hall of Fame History Book of Origin as a thank you; unless you decide to remain anonymous... We will also keep you updated on our progress and Ministries expansion. Once we launch our website you'll continue to be updated in case you'd like to follow us. We will have monthly pledges and one time contributions. UC Ministries will also be looking for volunteers for those called to serve.

On behalf of United by Christ, we would like to thank you for taking the time to look at our inspired Ministry through God the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit, Amen. Please keep us in your prayers.

With much love,
United by Christ Ministries
