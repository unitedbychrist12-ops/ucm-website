## EVENT PARTICIPATION STRATEGIES

### **SPONSORSHIP LEVELS**

**BRONZE LEVEL ($500-1,500)**
- **Logo on event materials** and website
- **Booth space** for volunteer recruitment
- **Donation collection** opportunities
- **Vacation incentive giveaways** for donations

**SILVER LEVEL ($1,500-5,000)**
- **Banner placement** at event
- **PA announcements** about UCM mission
- **VIP networking** opportunities
- **Social media mentions** by event organizers

**GOLD LEVEL ($5,000-15,000)**
- **Title sponsorship** opportunities
- **Speaking opportunities** about addiction recovery
- **Premium booth location**
- **Media interview** opportunities

### **BOOTH SETUP STRATEGY**

**VISUAL ELEMENTS:**
- **UCM banner** with mission statement
- **Success story displays** with photos and testimonials
- **Vacation package showcase** - visual displays of incentives
- **QR codes** for instant donations and vacation claims

**INTERACTIVE ELEMENTS:**
- **Donation station** with tablet for instant giving
- **Vacation wheel spin** - fun way to show incentive options
- **Photo booth** with UCM branding for social media
- **Information packets** with donation forms and R Save info

**STAFFING:**
- **2-3 volunteers** per event (trained on mission and incentives)
- **Bilingual staff** for Hispanic community events
- **Personal testimonies** from program graduates (powerful!)

## CROSS-SELLING OPPORTUNITIES

### **R SAVE INTEGRATION AT EVENTS**
**Dual Booth Setup:**
- **Left side:** UCM donation and mission information
- **Right side:** R Save services with vacation incentives
- **Shared messaging:** "Save money on services AND support a great cause"

**Event Attendee Approach:**
1. **Engage with UCM mission** - addiction recovery story
2. **Ask for donation** - with vacation incentive
3. **Mention R Save services** - "Also save money on your monthly bills"
4. **Collect contact info** - for follow-up on both UCM and R Save

### **LEAD GENERATION STRATEGY**
- **Business card collection** for Grace AI calling list
- **Service interest survey** - internet, solar, phones, etc.
- **Vacation incentive signup** - even if no immediate donation
- **Email list building** - for ongoing marketing
