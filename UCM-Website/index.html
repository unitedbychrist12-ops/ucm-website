Our Mission

"At United by Christ Ministries, our mission is to reduce homelessness and empower individuals to achieve holistic well-being by providing comprehensive 1, 2, and 3 step programs that address mental, physical, financial, and spiritual needs. Through compassionate support and guidance, we aim to restore hope and dignity, equipping those we serve to rejoin society as productive and fulfilled citizens, united by their faith and renewed purpose."

Our Story

United by Christ Ministries is dedicated to reach out to the Alcoholic, Drug Addict, Gang Member, Felon, Incarcerated, Homeless Adults & Families and any Souls in need of God's Love, Grace and Mercy.

Founded in 2018 by Aureliano "Audie" Reyes, UCM was born from personal experiences of restoration and renewal. Our founder once walked the difficult path of addiction and homelessness, finding hope through ministries like Barrios Unidos En Cristo.

Founder's Story

"I once found myself lost, searching desperately for answers and needing a drastic change in my life. My journey through addiction, gang involvement, and the streets had left me broken and without direction. Then one day, someone asked me a simple question that would change everything: 'How would you like to reach out to alcoholics, drug addicts, and gang members out in the streets?' Without hesitation, I replied, 'I would love to.'

That day marked the beginning of my transformation. I attended a Family Assembly with Barrios Unidos En Cristo at St. Athanasius Catholic Church in Long Beach, and there, God blessed me with my first profound encounter with our Lord Jesus Christ. In that moment of grace, I made a sacred commitment to God: to dedicate my life to helping others like me‚Äîpeople struggling to break free from the chains of alcohol, drugs, and violence.

The Holy Spirit immediately began a radical transformation in my life. I started serving in ministry, learning, growing, and experiencing the healing power of faith-based community. Through this journey, I discovered that true recovery requires more than just sobriety‚Äîit demands holistic restoration of mind, body, spirit, and purpose.

Years later, after facing my own trials and tribulations, God placed a calling on my heart that I could no longer ignore. On April 13, 2018, United by Christ Ministries was born‚Äînot just as an organization, but as a fulfillment of that sacred promise I made to God. Our mission is to provide the comprehensive support, unconditional love, and structured programs that I wish had been available during my darkest days."

‚Äî Aureliano "Audie" Reyes, Founder & CFO

Through United by Christ Ministries, God has bestowed upon us ambitious goals and a clear vision: to expand nationwide and eventually worldwide, helping every person and family in need discover the transformative power of faith, community, and comprehensive support.
