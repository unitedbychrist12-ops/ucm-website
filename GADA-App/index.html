What is G.A.D.A?

G.A.D.A is a comprehensive wellness app designed for anyone struggling with addiction - whether it's gambling, alcohol, drugs, or any other vice. Using the powerful Virtue vs Vice framework rooted in Catholic teaching, we provide a supportive community, progress tracking, and resources to help you achieve lasting sobriety and personal transformation.

Remember: Every human being has addictions. G.A.D.A is for anyone seeking to overcome their struggles through virtue and community support.

The Virtue vs Vice Framework

The 7 Virtues

  • Chastity - Purity in thought and action
  • Temperance - Self-control and moderation
  • Charity - Love and generosity
  • Diligence - Hard work and dedication
  • Patience - Endurance and forgiveness
  • Kindness - Compassion and empathy
  • Humility - Modesty and selflessness

The 7 Vices to Overcome

  • Lust - Overcome with Chastity
  • Gluttony - Overcome with Temperance
  • Greed - Overcome with Charity
  • Sloth - Overcome with Diligence
  • Wrath - Overcome with Patience
  • Envy - Overcome with Kindness
  • Pride - Overcome with Humility

By practicing virtues daily, you build strength to overcome the vices that lead to addiction and destructive behavior.

About G.A.D.A

G.A.D.A VvsV is a ministry of United by Christ Ministries (UCM), a 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to restoring lives through faith-based programs. We believe that every person struggling with addiction deserves support, community, and a path to lasting recovery.

The Virtue vs Vice framework, rooted in Catholic teaching, provides a proven method for overcoming destructive behaviors by replacing them with positive, life-giving practices.

Our Vision: To partner with AA, GA, NA, and other recovery organizations to provide a comprehensive digital platform that strengthens sobriety and builds lasting transformation.
