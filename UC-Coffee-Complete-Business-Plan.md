### **Path to Walmart Shelves**

#### **Prerequisites**
1. **Proven Sales History**: 6+ months of consistent 1,000+ bags/month
2. **Brand Recognition**: Strong social media presence and customer loyalty
3. **Quality Certifications**: FDA compliance, organic certifications if applicable
4. **Supply Chain Reliability**: Ability to fulfill large orders consistently

4. **Marketing Support**: Co-op advertising and promotional support
