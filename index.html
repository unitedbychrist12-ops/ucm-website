Our Mission

"At United by Christ Ministries, our mission is to reduce addiction and hardships by empowering individuals to achieve holistic well-being through our comprehensive 4-step program that addresses mental, physical, financial, and spiritual needs. Through compassionate support and guidance, we aim to restore hope and dignity, equipping those we serve to rejoin society as productive and fulfilled citizens, united by their faith and renewed purpose."

Our 4-Step Recovery Program

1

Mental Health & Emotional Wellness

Professional counseling and therapy to address mental health challenges, trauma, and addiction recovery with licensed therapists and doctors.

2

Physical Health & Medical Care

Complete health check-ups, medical treatment, dental care, nutrition counseling, and ongoing healthcare with doctors and nurses.

3

Financial Stability & Job Training

Financial counseling, job skills training, career planning, and support to achieve economic independence and stability.

4

Spiritual Growth & Life Purpose

Faith-based support, spiritual mentoring, community connection, and discovering your life's purpose through Christian ministry.

All services provided by licensed professionals with ongoing support and measurable outcomes.

Stories of Transformation

Real stories from people whose lives have been changed through UCM's programs

Audie Reyes - Founder & Executive Director

"My journey to founding UCM began in the darkest period of my life..."

After struggling with addiction and homelessness for years, I found myself at rock bottom. It was through the grace of God and the support of people who believed in second chances that I was able to turn my life around.

I experienced firsthand how addiction doesn't just affect the individual - it impacts families, communities, and society as a whole. But I also learned that with the right support system addressing mental, physical, financial, and spiritual needs, transformation is possible.

UCM was born from my desire to provide the comprehensive support I wish I had during my darkest days. We don't just treat symptoms - we address root causes and help people rebuild their entire lives.

Today, I'm blessed to lead an organization that has helped over 500 people find hope, healing, and purpose. Every person we serve reminds me why this work matters.

- Audie Reyes, Founder
