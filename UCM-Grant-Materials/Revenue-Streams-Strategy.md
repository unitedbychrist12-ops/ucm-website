## üõí REVENUE STREAM #4: RETAIL ROUND-UP PROGRAMS
**Checkout Donation Programs**

### Target Partners:

**Major Retailers:**
- Macy's
- Target
- Walmart
- Costco
- Home Depot
- Lowe's

**Grocery Stores:**
- Kroger
- Safeway
- Albertsons
- Whole Foods
- Trader Joe's

**Restaurants:**
- Chipotle
- Panera Bread
- Local restaurant chains

### How It Works:
1. Customer checks out
2. Cashier/system asks: "Round up for UCM?"
3. Customer rounds up to nearest dollar
4. Donation goes to UCM
5. Customer gets tax receipt

### Revenue Potential:
- **Single store:** $5K - $20K annually
- **100 stores:** $500K - $2M annually
- **1,000 stores:** $5M - $20M annually

### Strategy:
- Start with 1-2 pilot partners
- Prove model with data
- Scale to major chains
- Provide marketing materials
- Share impact stories
