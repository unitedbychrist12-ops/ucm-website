## üéØ TARGET CALIFORNIA GRANTS

### **1. California Emergency Solutions and Housing (CESH) Program**
- **Amount:** $500K - $5M+
- **Focus:** Homelessness prevention and rapid rehousing
- **Deadline:** Typically annual (check HCD website)
- **Website:** https://www.hcd.ca.gov/grants-and-funding/programs-active/cesh
- **Perfect For:** UCM's housing and recovery programs
- **Application Tips:**
  - Emphasize 3-step program outcomes
  - Show partnership with local governments
  - Demonstrate measurable success metrics

### **2. Homeless Housing, Assistance and Prevention (HHAP) Program**
- **Amount:** $1M - $10M+
- **Focus:** Comprehensive homeless services
- **Deadline:** Varies by round
- **Website:** https://www.bcsh.ca.gov/hcfc/hhap.html
- **Perfect For:** Phase 1 hotel acquisition
- **Application Tips:**
  - Highlight holistic approach (mental, physical, spiritual)
  - Show long-term case management (5 years)
  - Include detox and vocational training components

### **3. CalWORKs Housing Support Program**
- **Amount:** $100K - $2M
- **Focus:** Housing for families with children
- **Deadline:** Rolling applications
- **Website:** https://www.cdss.ca.gov/inforesources/calworks
- **Perfect For:** UC Families ministry
- **Application Tips:**
  - Focus on family formation programs
  - Show childcare and education components
  - Demonstrate job placement success

### **4. Substance Abuse Prevention and Treatment Block Grant**
- **Amount:** $250K - $3M
- **Focus:** Addiction treatment and recovery
- **Deadline:** Annual
- **Website:** https://www.dhcs.ca.gov/services/sapt
- **Perfect For:** UC Recovery and detox center
- **Application Tips:**
  - Emphasize evidence-based treatment
  - Show partnership with licensed facilities
  - Include virtue vs vice framework as innovative approach

### **5. California Nonprofit Security Grant Program**
- **Amount:** $50K - $200K
- **Focus:** Security improvements for nonprofits
- **Deadline:** Annual
- **Website:** https://www.caloes.ca.gov/cal-oes-divisions/grants-management
- **Perfect For:** Facility security systems
- **Application Tips:**
  - Detail 24/7 surveillance needs
  - Show security guard requirements
  - Emphasize vulnerable population protection

### **6. Community Development Block Grant (CDBG)**
- **Amount:** $100K - $1M+
- **Focus:** Community development and housing
- **Deadline:** Varies by county
- **Contact:** Local city/county government
- **Perfect For:** Facility renovation and construction
- **Application Tips:**
  - Work with city of Oceanside/San Diego
  - Show job creation (staff positions)
  - Demonstrate community benefit

### **7. California Endowment Health Equity Grants**
- **Amount:** $50K - $500K
- **Focus:** Health equity and community wellness
- **Deadline:** Rolling
- **Website:** https://www.calendow.org/
- **Perfect For:** Mental health and wellness programs
- **Application Tips:**
  - Focus on underserved populations
  - Show health outcome improvements
  - Include G.A.D.A app as innovative tool

### **8. Kaiser Permanente Community Health Grants**
- **Amount:** $25K - $250K
- **Focus:** Community health and housing
- **Deadline:** Quarterly
- **Website:** https://about.kaiserpermanente.org/community-health
- **Perfect For:** Health services in recovery programs
- **Application Tips:**
  - Partner with local Kaiser facilities
  - Show measurable health outcomes
  - Include mental health components

### **9. Wells Fargo Housing Foundation**
- **Amount:** $100K - $1M
- **Focus:** Affordable housing and homelessness
- **Deadline:** Annual
- **Website:** https://www.wellsfargo.com/about/corporate-responsibility/community-giving
- **Perfect For:** Housing acquisition and development
- **Application Tips:**
  - Emphasize housing stability outcomes
  - Show financial literacy programs
  - Include job placement support

### **10. Bank of America Charitable Foundation**
- **Amount:** $50K - $500K
- **Focus:** Housing and economic mobility
- **Deadline:** Rolling
- **Website:** https://about.bankofamerica.com/en/making-an-impact/charitable-foundation-funding
- **Perfect For:** Comprehensive recovery programs
- **Application Tips:**
  - Show workforce development
  - Demonstrate economic self-sufficiency
  - Include financial education

## üéØ GRANT WRITING STRATEGY

### **Phase 1: Immediate (0-3 months)**
1. **Apply for smaller grants first ($25K-$100K)**
   - Build track record
   - Get testimonials
   - Establish credibility

2. **Target grants:**
   - Kaiser Permanente Community Health
   - California Endowment (smaller tier)
   - Bank of America (initial funding)

### **Phase 2: Foundation Building (3-6 months)**
1. **Apply for medium grants ($100K-$500K)**
   - Show initial success
   - Demonstrate program effectiveness
   - Build partnerships

2. **Target grants:**
   - CalWORKs Housing Support
   - CDBG (county level)
   - Wells Fargo Housing Foundation

### **Phase 3: Major Funding (6-12 months)**
1. **Apply for large grants ($500K-$5M+)**
   - Present proven model
   - Show measurable outcomes
   - Demonstrate scalability

2. **Target grants:**
   - CESH Program
   - HHAP Program
   - SAPT Block Grant

## üí° WINNING GRANT STRATEGIES

### **1. Tell Compelling Stories**
- Use founder testimonials (Audie & Brooke)
- Include participant success stories
- Show before/after transformations
- Use photos and videos

### **2. Show Measurable Impact**
- 500+ individuals served annually
- 70%+ program completion rate
- 85%+ job placement rate
- 75%+ sustained housing after 2 years

### **3. Demonstrate Innovation**
- Virtue vs Vice framework (unique approach)
- G.A.D.A app (technology integration)
- 5-year case management (long-term support)
- Holistic model (mental, physical, financial, spiritual)

### **4. Build Strong Partnerships**
- Local government (city/county)
- Healthcare providers
- Educational institutions
- Faith communities
- Business sector (R Companies model)

### **5. Show Financial Sustainability**
- R Companies LLC (80% profit donation)
- Individual donor base
- Corporate sponsorships
- Service fees (where appropriate)
- Product sales
