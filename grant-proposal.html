Executive Summary

United by Christ Ministries (UCM) is seeking funding to establish comprehensive homeless recovery facilities that address the root causes of homelessness through holistic programs encompassing mental, physical, financial, and spiritual restoration. Our innovative 3-step program has the potential to serve 500+ individuals annually with measurable outcomes and long-term success rates.

Mission Statement

"At United by Christ Ministries, our mission is to reduce homelessness and empower individuals to achieve holistic well-being by providing comprehensive 1, 2, and 3 step programs that address mental, physical, financial, and spiritual needs. Through compassionate support and guidance, we aim to restore hope and dignity, equipping those we serve to rejoin society as productive and fulfilled citizens, united by their faith and renewed purpose."

Innovative Approach: Mobile Outreach RV

Before establishing permanent facilities, UCM will deploy a mobile shower RV unit to:

  • Provide immediate hygiene services to homeless individuals
  • Build trust and relationships with the street population
  • Document individual stories and testimonials
  • Create comprehensive case files for each person
  • Capture video testimonials showing the human face of homelessness
  • Build compelling evidence for major donors and government grants
  • Identify individuals ready for comprehensive program enrollment

The 3-Step Program

Step 1: Stabilization & Detox (30-90 days)

  • Medical assessment and detoxification
  • Mental health evaluation
  • Case manager assignment (1:10-20 ratio)
  • Basic needs provision (housing, food, hygiene)
  • Introduction to faith-based support

Step 2: Education & Skill Development (6-9 months)

  • Vocational training programs
  • Job readiness workshops
  • Financial literacy education
  • Continued counseling and therapy
  • Faith formation classes
  • Job placement assistance

Step 3: Independent Living Support (3-12 months)

  • Housing placement assistance
  • 6 months rent coverage safety net
  • Ongoing case management (weekly ‚Üí monthly ‚Üí bi-annual)
  • Employment support and monitoring
  • Community reintegration
  • Opportunity to give back through volunteering

Measurable Objectives

  • Serve 500+ homeless individuals annually
  • Achieve 70%+ program completion rate
  • 85%+ job placement rate for graduates
  • 75%+ sustained housing after 2 years
  • Documented mental health improvement in 90%+ participants
